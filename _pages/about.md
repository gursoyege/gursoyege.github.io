I am a postdoctoral researcher at LAAS-CNRS in Toulouse, France, where I am a member of the Gepetto team working on robot learning. My research focuses on robotic manipulation, model predictive control, robot perception, and learning-based methods for robotics. I organize Gepetto Science Seminars and coordinate invited speakers. I am also an associate editor for IROS.

[Email](mailto:egursoy@laas.fr){: .btn .btn--primary} [Publications](/publications/){: .btn} [Teaching](/teaching/){: .btn} [CV](/cv/){: .btn}

Research Interests
======

- Robot learning for manipulation
- Model predictive control and constrained control
- Dual-arm manipulation and robotic fruit harvesting
- Vision-based manipulation in deformable and cluttered environments
- Verification, out-of-distribution detection, and certified learning for robotics
